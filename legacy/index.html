About Us

We Help you build your Legacy by passing down your story to your descendants.

We are you! We spent hours on other sites looking for a glimpse into the past. After all that research we had a list of names and where they were born and died, and this just wasn’t enough for us.

What did your great grandpa think about WW1 or WW2 for that matter? What was your grandma’s reaction to the 19th amendment? This would have been priceless information that could be passed down for generations.

The staff here at Legacy is committed to amplifying your voice to your decedents. Giving you the ability to share your story even if you’re not there. We hope you enjoy your experience and leave your Legacy.
